Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Prepaid Credits

TokenHub operates on a prepaid credit model. When you purchase credits, we activate the equivalent amount in your account balance immediately.

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

  • Within 14 days of purchase — Full refund on unused credits. If any credits have been consumed, those are deducted.
  • Service outage — If our service is unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours, you may request a pro-rata refund.
  • Duplicate charges — Full refund immediately.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Credits consumed through API usage (even if you are unsatisfied with the output)
  • Account termination due to Terms of Service violation
  • Purchases older than 14 days

4. How to Request

Email refunds@middlekingdom.cc with your account email and purchase details. We process refunds within 5 business days.

5. Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your card issuer before contacting us, your account may be suspended pending resolution.
