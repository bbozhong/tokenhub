Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What We Collect

When you sign up, we collect your email address. When you use our API, we log request metadata (timestamps, model used, token count) for billing and monitoring. We do not log the content of your API requests.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To calculate usage and charges
  • To monitor and improve performance
  • To communicate with you about your account

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Upstream Providers — Your API requests are forwarded to the AI model providers you choose
  • Payment Processors — Paddle handles payment transactions; we do not store credit card information
  • Legal Requirements — If required by law

4. Data Retention

Usage logs are retained for 90 days. Account data is retained until you request deletion.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
